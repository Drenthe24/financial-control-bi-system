# KPI Definitions

## EBITDA
Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation and Amortization.

Used to measure operational profitability excluding financial and accounting effects.

## Gross Profit
Total revenues minus cost of goods sold.

Indicates the profitability of core business activities.

## Net Financial Position (PFN)
Difference between financial debts and liquid assets.

Used to assess company financial stability and liquidity.

## Cash Flow
Net change in cash over a period.

Essential to monitor liquidity and short-term sustainability.

## Cost Drivers
Top cost categories impacting profitability.

Used to identify areas for optimization.

## Revenue Drivers
Top revenue sources.

Used to understand business performance and growth drivers.
