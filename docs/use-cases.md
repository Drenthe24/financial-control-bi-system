# Use Cases

## Overview

This document describes how the financial control system supports real business decisions.

## Cash Flow Monitoring

The system allows continuous monitoring of cash flow and liquidity trends.

Decision impact:
- identify liquidity risks early
- plan short-term financial needs
- improve cash management

## Cost Control

By analyzing cost drivers, the system highlights the most impactful cost categories.

Decision impact:
- identify inefficiencies
- optimize operational expenses
- support cost reduction strategies

## Revenue Analysis

The system provides visibility on revenue composition and trends.

Decision impact:
- identify growth areas
- evaluate performance over time
- support pricing and commercial decisions

## Profitability Analysis

KPIs such as EBITDA and margins allow evaluation of business performance.

Decision impact:
- assess operational efficiency
- compare performance across periods
- support strategic planning

## Financial Position Monitoring

The Net Financial Position provides a clear view of financial stability.

Decision impact:
- evaluate debt sustainability
- support financing decisions
- monitor financial risk
